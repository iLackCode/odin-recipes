Nachos

Description

Nachos are a nice finger food that can have many different toppings they are easy to make and even the most basic nachos can fill one up The nachos we will make here are made to feed a family! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground meat of choice
  • 2 green onions
  • 3 roma tomatoes
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1 taco seasoning
  • 16 oz cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 400 degrees
  2. on stovetop, brown the ground beef and diced onions over medium high heat until fully cooked
  3. return the beef to pan, and sprinkle on the taco seasoning
  4. stir in 3/4 cup water, bring it to a simmer and reduce heat and simmer until the liquid mostly evaporates
  5. while the beef is cooking, add a generous layer of chips to two baking sheets lined with parchment paper
  6. spoon on refried beans, half on each pan. these are thick add spoon fulls over the chips
  7. spoon on half the ground beef to each pan of chips
  8. sprinkle shredded cheese on top
  9. bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes
  10. remove from the oven and sprinkle on diced tomatoes and green onions
  11. serve immediately
