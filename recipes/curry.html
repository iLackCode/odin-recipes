Curry

Description

Curry is a staple in many differnt cultures and blends flavors and spices in a way that is unimaginable. This curry is a japnese curry I learned from a friend!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb protein of choice
  • 13 oz onions
  • 3.5 oz carrots
  • 5 oz potatoes
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 1/4 water
  • 1 box S&B Golden Curry

Curry is great with rice

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • stove top pot

SIDE NOTE curry is great with a variety of different ingredients, feel free to have some fun with what you throw in yours. Apples, sweet potatoes, etc try a bunch

Steps

  1. Pour cooking oil in big pot and throw chopped veggies in
  2. While watching your veggies, begin cooking your protein
  3. Once protein cooked, strain or remove from pan and add to now cooked veggies
  4. Turn heat up and add water in to pot with all ingredients
  5. Bring all to a good boil and cover the pot for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally
  6. After lower heat to lo and break S&B Golden Curry packet and spread evenly into pot mixing it all together
  7. stir until all seasoning is evenly spread and no lumps are found
  8. let sit for 5 minutes
  9. Serve
Rice Bonus
  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Pour rice in
  3. Cover for 10-15 minutes
  4. Serve
